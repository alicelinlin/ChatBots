PROJECT MANAGER

By Alice Wen Lin

About us

Technology is bringing healthcare into the digital era and becoming more integrated into everyone's daily lives. The rise of Covid-19 brought new challenges to healthcare services around the world ever, bringing ever-so uncertain futures. With the help of and cutting-edge AI and broader technologies, we can confront these implications in a different light. We're here to help.

The Health-Care Chatbots RMIT offers new innovative approaches for individuals to access health databases and help patients get answers for their questions, and assess symptoms related to coronavirus. If successful, this will significantly reduce workloads at hospitals and health care facilities, reducing unessential hospital visits and decreasing overall hospital admissions. However, the goal of this project will not be limited to Covid-19 and will expand to all areas of the medical industry, including mental health.

Our company's vision is to merge cutting-edge AI and technology with the best IT experts and healthcare professionals to re-engineer a better healthcare model. Our mission is to provide open-source, affordable, and accessible health care in the hands of every citizen on Earth.

Job Description

The Chatbots RMIT seeks to hire a Project Manager dedicated to helping us create and improve our vision. You will be the main driver for the execution and completion of the project, working with the team and customers to ensure project success. The ideal candidate will be flexible, well-organized, and detail-oriented with excellent interpersonal and communication skills. The Project Manager must demonstrate their performance adherence to established company protocols and guidelines.

The position we are offering is a project manager that is in charge of overseeing every aspect of the Covid-19 Chatbot Project. You will be accountable for the entirety of the project, which includes the driving of day-to-day activities, project planning, scope, and schedules, managing the configuration and deployment of the project, and Project Integrations.

Other areas such as financials and budget control, product quality and safety controls, and detailed documentation of the project process are also required to ensure project completion within specific time frames, budget, and resources available. You will be the single point contact for the accountability for all aspects of the assigned projects.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Oversee the implementation and completion of the project, assist team members in maintaining an overarching project strategy, and supporting project plans.
  • Leadership and Communication: Effectively communicate and with team members and stakeholders in a timely and consistent manner, manage objectives consistently, and maintain single-point accountability.
  • Developing and executing plans: Plan, schedule, manage, and track project timelines and milestones using appropriate tools/technology and resources, including Microsoft Teams and Office Suites 365
  • IaaS platforms: Working with IaaS platforms such as Azure AD, Azure Bot, Azure Health ChatBot, GitHub, and other two-way messaging AI softwares
  • multi-tasking: Engage in several activities simultaneously
  • Refine project execution with the best project management principles and standards. Proactively identifying changes and potential obstacles with projects and developing contingency plans.
  • Documentation: Develop trackable documentation with detailed feedback and progress reports, including time/action logs, project status, risk and liability reports.
  • Troubleshooting open issues and bug-fixes with ChatBot prototype; prototype tests and reviews, consistently enhancing design to prevent reoccurrences of defects; offer continuous amendments and improvements for better project delivery.
  • At the project's completion, provide an overview of the project's accomplishments, including team feedback, challenges, lessons learnt.
